Cleaned-up, HTML-ified, Archived

There’s a lot of great stuff on the ol’ interwebs, but it’s not always presented in a nice, easy-to-read fashion. Some of it pre-dates mobile devices with small screens, and some of it has extraneous formatting or other stuff. Sometimes, things are not even in HTML (gasp!). So, I sometimes fix that. Plus it’s good to keep copies of valuable things, in case they fall off the web, as sometimes happens.

  • Notes On The Culture, by Iain M. Banks
  • The Emperor’s Old Clothes, by C. A. R. Hoare
  • How To Work, by Jacob Reimer
  • In The Beginning Was The Command Line, by Neal Stephenson
