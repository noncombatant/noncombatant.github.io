Florilegium

18 December 2024

About 11 months after I thought for sure we’d be done, my band Telltale Shimmer released our new EP, Florilegium! I’m pretty happy with how it turned out. I think it’s a decent representation of what I was trying to achieve, which I call “Discipline-era Cocteau Twins”. If you dig it, we’re working on a new batch of tunes. Ideally, it’ll come out in less than a year...
