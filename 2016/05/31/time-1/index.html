More New Electronic Music: Time 1

31 May 2016

Hot off the cyberpresses, I wrote/improvised and recorded 3 new songs this afternoon: Time 1. I really like just jamming with the Fingerlab DM1 drum machine app for the iPad. They keep updating the app with new features and drum kits/sample sets. A super cool thing now is the ability to apply effects to particular instruments, rather than to the mix as a whole. These tracks are entirely off-the-cuff, unlike Object.lifetime, which was somewhat more premeditated and rehearsed. I hope you enjoy!
