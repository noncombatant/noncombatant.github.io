Cryptographically Random Tone Rows

4 August 2013 04:12 UTC

This program prints cryptographically random tone rows.

The scotch was obviously Macallan 17. Released under the terms of the GNU General Public License version 1 (only).

Usage:

12 [-h] [-r random-pathname] [number-of-rows]

$ 12 2
A♮  G♭  A♭  B♮  F♮  G♮  B♭  D♭  D♮  E♭  C♮  E♮  
B♭  E♮  B♮  G♮  E♭  A♭  D♭  G♭  F♮  D♮  C♮  A♮
